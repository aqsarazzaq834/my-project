Research Proposal

Applied Mathematical Models for Predicting Climate-Induced Disasters

My research focuses on developing mathematical and computational models to predict disasters influenced by climate and seismic activity. The aim is to integrate climate factors (temperature, rainfall, sea-level rise) with seismic data (earthquake frequency, magnitude) to create accurate prediction systems.

  • Data Sources: Climate (JMA, NOAA, IPCC), Seismic (USGS, Japan Seismic DB), GIS maps.
  • Methods: Stochastic climate models, probabilistic seismic hazard models, machine learning calibration.
  • Outcomes: Risk maps, dynamic early-warning thresholds, disaster preparedness strategies.
